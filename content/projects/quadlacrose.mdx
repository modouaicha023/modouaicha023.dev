## Project Overview

Quad Lac Rose is a professionally developed website using **Next.js**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **TypeScript**. Designed for a local tour company in Senegal, the platform showcases a variety of tours and experiences around the iconic Pink Lake (*Lac Rose*), allowing users to easily explore and book online.

This project was completed as part of a **freelance collaboration**, focusing on usability, responsiveness, and fast performance.
