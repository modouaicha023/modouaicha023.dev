**K.wax Design** is a handmade home furniture brand based in Dakar, Senegal. The company blends traditional craftsmanship with vibrant African wax fabric to create bold, customized pieces for interior spaces.

Each item is 100% handmade, and customers can personalize their designs to bring a unique and colorful energy into their homes.

## 🪑 What They Offer

- Handmade furniture, 100% made in Senegal  
- Fully customizable designs  
- Unique pieces using African wax fabric  
- A fresh, colorful twist on traditional home decor
